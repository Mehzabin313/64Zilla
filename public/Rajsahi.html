Rajshahi – The Land of Sweet Mangoes

Rajshahi, often referred to as the "Silk City" and the "Education City" of Bangladesh, is a serene urban center situated on the northern banks of the mighty Padma River. Renowned for its clean, green, and quiet atmosphere, the city stands out as one of the most livable urban areas in the country. It is globally recognized for its high-quality silk industry and is equally famous for being the "Mango Capital," producing the finest varieties of the fruit during the summer. Historically, Rajshahi is a treasure trove of heritage, housing the Varendra Research Museum—the oldest of its kind in the country—and the nearby architectural marvels of the Puthia Rajbari. With its prestigious educational institutions like Rajshahi University, a breezy riverfront that offers breathtaking sunsets, and a culture rooted in politeness and intellect, Rajshahi remains a vital hub of history, education, and natural beauty.
