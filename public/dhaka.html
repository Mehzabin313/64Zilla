Dhaka – The City of Weaving Heritage

Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh, is the country's political, economic, and cultural hub. It is one of the most densely populated cities in the world, known for its vibrant street life, historical landmarks, and bustling markets. The city blends modernity with tradition, featuring colonial-era architecture alongside contemporary buildings. Dhaka is famous for its rich culinary culture, including traditional Bengali dishes and street food. Key attractions include Lalbagh Fort, Ahsan Manzil, and the National Museum. The city also hosts major events, festivals, and a thriving textile industry, making it central to Bangladesh’s economy and heritage.
