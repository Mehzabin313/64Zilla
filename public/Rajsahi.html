Tangail – The City of Weaving Heritage

Tangail is one of the most vibrant and culturally rich districts of Bangladesh, lovingly known as the “City of Weaving.” The pride of Tangail is its world-famous Tangail Saree, a handwoven masterpiece that reflects the artistry, patience, and tradition of its people. For generations, this weaving heritage has carried the name of Tangail far beyond the borders of Bangladesh. But Tangail is not only about weaving. The district is also famous for its mouthwatering Chamcham sweets, lush green fields, and the scenic beauty of the Madhupur Garh forest, home to the majestic sal trees and diverse wildlife. Its natural charm blends perfectly with the warmth and hospitality of its people. Located close to Dhaka, Tangail serves as a bridge between tradition and modernity. It stands as a symbol of creativity, culture, and resilience — a place where history lives on through every thread of the loom and every flavor of its sweets.
